Frequently Asked Questions

How much does it cost to travel by bus ?

Travel by bus is the way cheap to travelling, you can enter your destination and search to see more details about the cost and other information.

Do I have to keep printed ticket?

It is not necessary to have your ticket printed. Whenever you finish buying ticket you receive an email which is valid for taking a ride.

Can I choose my bus seat?

It’s more convenient with seats. When you buy ticket, you’ll be given a seat number. However, a customer can choose seat number depending on specific case with extra fees.

Can I cancel my booking?

Yes, whenever you change your mind you can cancel your bus booking with in a specified timeframe following the specified terms and conditions.

Is there a baggage allowance for bus travel? Are there any restrictions on luggage size or weight?

Yes, there is usually a baggage allowance for bus travel, we allow passengers to carry a certain amount of luggage, which may include both checked baggage stored in the bus's compartment and carry-on items that can be taken onboard.

How do I book a bus ride on iRwandaTickets?

We have tried our best to make booking ticket the way easy for our clients. to book_ you just navigate to booking tickets in menu items at right side navigation list items.

with iRwandaTickets you can choose your destination and tell us the day you will travel and the number of tickets you want.
